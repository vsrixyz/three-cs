# The Three Sees of Content Design

The three Cs of content design are targets for good content design work. 

The three sees must proceed in order:

1. Make the elements CONSISTENT 
2. Tell a COHESIVE story
3. Let the message become CLEAR

These principles help answer fundamental questions about what makes good content design good, how to measure success, and what the purpose of content design is. These are targets rather than rules.
